The idea of the blog is to talk about running a website yourself and optimizing it for search engines. I hope to make it clear but refrain from unnecessary simplifications so that the information doesn’t lose its practical sense. It’s not about promoting the site on social networks but about mutual understanding with search bots and ranking algorithms. Dedicated to all who:

  • prefers to go into the matter in depth rather than often ask for help
  • understands that traffic increase requires careful preparation
  • has the enthusiasm to win the Search competition and get user clicks.
