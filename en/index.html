Some SEO theory and DIY tricks

The idea of the blog is to talk about running a website yourself and optimizing it for search engines.   • prefers to go into the matter in depth rather than often ask for help
  • understands that traffic increase requires careful preparation
  • has the enthusiasm to win the Search competition and get user clicks.
